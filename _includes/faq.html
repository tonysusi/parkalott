FAQs
How does PARKALOTT work?
PARKALOTT is a web-based application which your staff download onto their smartphones and then use to manage your workplace parking.
Does PARKALOTT need equipment?
PARKALOTT is operated by smartphones, so there’s no need to install hardware on-site.
Can we continue using PARKALOTT after the 3-month trial?
Yes. You simply carry on using PARKALOTT as you have been and then pay a monthly fee dependent on the size of your business.
What if we don’t want to continue using PARKALOTT after the 3-month trial?
No problem, we’ll switch off your business’s access to PARKALOTT and there’s no cost to you.
